## On-Page SEO Findings

### Title Tags
- **Issue:** Title tag acceptable but could be more optimized for primary keywords
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Evidence:** "Carroll Chevrolet, Toyota Dealer in Carroll IA | Roselle Wiley Glidden Toyota Dealership Iowa"
  - Includes location and brands (good)
  - Could lead with primary keyword target like "Used Cars for Sale in Carroll IA | Motor Inn Auto Group"
  - Length appears OK (~70 characters)
- **Fix:** A/B test title variations focusing on priority keywords from context (used [make] [model] Iowa, car dealership Carroll Iowa)
- **Priority:** Medium

### Meta Descriptions
- **Issue:** Description is accurate but lacks compelling call-to-action and unique value proposition
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Evidence:** "Motor Inn Auto Group of Carroll IA serving Roselle, Wiley, Glidden, is one of the finest Carroll Toyota dealers."
  - Mentions service area (Roselle, Wiley, Glidden) - good for local relevance
  - "finest" is subjective; could be more specific/trust-building
  - Missing clear value proposition (price, selection, service quality) or CTA
- **Fix:** Rewrite to include: location, inventory strength, trust signal (90 years), and soft CTA
  Example: "Family-owned since 1934. Motor Inn Auto Group offers trusted Chevrolet & Toyota sales, service & parts in Carroll IA. See our inventory or schedule service today."
- **Priority:** Medium

### Heading Structure
- **Issue:** H1 tag has formatting error - missing space
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Evidence:** `h1: ["Welcome toMotor Inn Auto Group"]` - concatenation error likely in CMS/template
- **Fix:** Correct template to add space: "Welcome to Motor Inn Auto Group"
- **Priority:** High (easy fix, affects accessibility and SEO)

### Content Optimization
- **Issue:** Homepage appears to be primarily navigational/banner-driven with minimal unique text content
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Evidence:** Word count: 208 words (low for homepage); heavy reliance on banners/images for vehicle promotion
- **Fix:** Consider adding crawlable text content that reinforces E-E-A-T: 
  - Brief history (90 years, family-owned)
  - Service area description
  - Value proposition (Transparent pricing, local accountability)
  - Brand-specific sections (what makes their Chevrolet/Toyota experience unique)
- **Priority:** Medium

### Image Optimization
- **Issue:** Several images missing width and height attributes
- **Impact:** Medium (contributes to Cumulative Layout Shift)
- **Evidence:** Multiple images in parsed output show `"width": null, "height": null`
- **Fix:** Add width/height attributes to all images in CMS/template where possible
- **Priority:** Medium (easy fix, improves Core Web Vitals)

### Internal Linking
- **Issue:** Internal link structure appears solid
- **Impact:** Low
- **Evidence:** Numerous internal links to key sections (inventory, service, parts, about, etc.)
- **Fix:** No action needed
- **Priority:** Low
